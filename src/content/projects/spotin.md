Note: The codebase belongs to a private customer and is not publicly available. 

SpotIn was an ecotourism startup I co-founded with two close friends during college. My primary contribution was the design and development of a nature-discovery mobile application built with Swift 3.0. The app featured Google Maps integration, location-based discovery of nearby natural attractions, fuzzy search, and a bookmarking system that allowed users to save destinations and plan future adventures.
